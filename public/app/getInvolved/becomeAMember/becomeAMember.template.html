ABOUT US

WHAT WE DO

As an organization, we:

  • Volunteer in our local community
  • Volunteer abroad on our medical mission trips
  • Fundraise for local philanthropies like Dance Marathon and Relay for Life
  • Host workshops for useful tools for college students with an interest in the health field
  • Meet and interact with other members of the Gainesville health community

And so much more!

We stand together to advocate for quality universal healthcare in a fun and educational environment.
