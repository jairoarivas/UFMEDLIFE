FOR MEMBERS

To be considered an active member you must earn 15 points per semester:

Must have: 2 service events, 2 social events, 2 fundraiser events, 1 workshop events, 1 philanthropy event

Remaining points can be from any other types of events listed above. (Certain events will carry more points).

HOW TO EARN POINTS

  1. Sign up and pay for membership: 1 point
  2. Philanthropy donations will add 1 point requirement (assigned accordingly: DM/Relay/food cans)
  3. Director for a day/liaison for day (event suggestions/program connection): 3 points
  4. Points per type of event:
    1. Service:
      • 1 hour = 1 point
      • 2 hour = 3 point
      • Per additional service hour = 1 point
    2. Social: 1 point
      • Additional points (max 3): for any food contribution/Venmo donations
    3. Fundraiser:
      • 1 point/1 hour shift
      • 3 point/2 hour shifts
    4. Philanthropy:
      • 4 points if participating in a team
      • 2 point max: Food cans/non-perishable goods
    5. Workshops: 1 point per event
    6. Committee member (active participation): 1 point/month of involvement
